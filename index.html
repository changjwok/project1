Critical Challenges in Juba

Based on recent research and local entrepreneur testimonies, these are the primary obstacles facing small businesses today.

Hyperinflation & Currency Volatility

SSP lost 60% of value since 2020. Prices change daily, wiping out savings and making pricing impossible.

View Solution

Multiple Taxation

Businesses taxed by City Council, CID, state and national agencies simultaneously without unified system.

View Solution

Limited Access to Finance

Only 30 registered banks operate nationwide, mostly in Juba. No international transfers possible.

View Solution

Poor Infrastructure

Unreliable electricity, limited internet, poor transport routes, and lack of storage facilities.

View Solution

Insecurity & Theft

Armed roadblocks, theft of goods and harvests, property rights violations, and arbitrary fees.

View Solution

Lack of Business Skills

Poor financial management, lack of digital literacy, and limited access to training programs.

View Solution

About Juba Business Hub

We are dedicated to empowering small businesses in South Sudan by providing practical solutions to the unique challenges faced in Juba's volatile market environment.

Our Mission

To create a supportive ecosystem where Juba's entrepreneurs can thrive despite economic instability, infrastructure challenges, and regulatory complexities.

What We Do

  • Provide daily market intelligence and pricing tools
  • Offer training in business skills and financial literacy
  • Connect entrepreneurs with alternative financing options
  • Advocate for better business policies and regulations
  • Build community networks for mutual support
